BMW, or Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, is a renowned German multinational corporation specializing in luxury vehicles and motorcycles. Established in 1916, BMW has built a global reputation for its high-performance cars, innovative technology, and stylish designs. Headquartered in Munich, Germany, BMW operates manufacturing facilities worldwide. The company offers a diverse range of vehicles, including sedans, coupes, convertibles, SUVs, and electric cars under its "i" sub-brand, as well as motorcycles under BMW Motorrad. With a focus on engineering excellence and driving experience, BMW has become synonymous with luxury and performance in the automotive industry.
